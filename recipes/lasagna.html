Lasagna

Provided by Pexels

Description

A delicious beef lasagna with a cheesy garnish

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products (sauce, paste, crushed)
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings (parsley, basil, salt, pepper)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese (parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta)
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook beef and mix with tomato products to make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
