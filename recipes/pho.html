Description

Bowl of beef noodle soup

Ingredients

  • Pho noodles
  • Beef bone
  • Beef brisket
  • Encrusted beef
  • Beef shank
  • Beef ball
  • Beef flank
  • Cinnamon
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Star anise
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Fish sauce
  • Beansprouts
  • Basil
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Chilli sauce
  • Chilli
  • Water
  1. Clean all the beef ingredients with salt water
  2. Boil the beef ingredients in water to release its dirt
  3. Heat the garlic, cinnamon, and onion on a pan for aroma
  4. Put the pan ingredients and star anise into a pot and then add all the beef on top, and fill with water
  5. Boil for six hours
  6. Season the pot with salt, sugar, and fish sauce
  7. Serve the dish with pho noodles, all kinds of diced beef, diced onions, diced green onion, hoisin sauce, chilli sauce, diced chilli with a side of beansprouts and basil
