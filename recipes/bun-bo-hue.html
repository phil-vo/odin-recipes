Bun Bo Hue

Provided by Wikimedia Commons

Description

Traditional Vietnamese noodle soup

Ingredients

  • Beef bone
  • Beef shank
  • Oxtail
  • Pork hocks
  • Hue pork sausage
  • Block of pork blood
  • Sate
  • Dried rice noodle
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Lemongrass
  • Banana flower
  • Bean sprouts
  • Mint
  • Basil
  • Lime
  • Water
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Monosodium glutamate (MSG)
  • Korean chile powder (gochugaru)
  • Cooking wine

Steps

  1. Clean the meat with salt water
  2. Boil meat in hot water and cooking wine to release the dirt
  3. Steam the beef bone, beef shank, oxtail, and pork hocks for two hours
  4. Add sugar, salt, MSG, and lemongrass in the beef pot
  5. Boil the pork blood with a little bit of garlic in a separate pot
  6. Add a drizzle of oil, garlic, onion, grinded lemongrass, and Hue shrimp paste on a pan until aromatic, then add korean chile powder
  7. Put the seasoning mixture into the beef pot
  8. Boil noodle in a separate pot
  9. Prepare the dishes
  10. Add noodles, diced beef shank, oxtail, pork hocks, Hue pork sausage, block of pork blood, diced onions, and pour the beef stock into the bowl
  11. Serve with the Bun Bo Hue bowl with a side of vegetable platter
